About Us

Welcome to Dreams Trust

Dreams Trust, registered under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, is dedicated to transforming lives through education, health, women and child development, and environmental sustainability. With our 80G status, donors can avail tax benefits on their contributions. Our DIN No is AAETD1231EF2024101, and PAN No is AAETD1231E. At Dreams Trust, we're committed to supporting child education by providing school accessories and organizing educational programs. Our health camps, aimed at underserved areas, strive to improve access to healthcare services. Through our women and child development initiatives, we empower women and ensure the well-being of children. Additionally, our social work projects and plantation drives underscore our dedication to environmental conservation. Join us at Dreams Trust as we work towards creating positive change and nurturing dreams for a better tomorrow.

Our History

August 2020

In August 2020, a group of young individuals came together with a common purpose: to help people. Though not officially registered, our commitment to social work and child education drives us forward. Our motto is clear: to make a difference in people's lives through kindness and education.

March 2021

In 2021, Dreams Trust planted 150 trees in our native area, championing local environmental sustainability. This initiative reflects our commitment to nurturing greener communities and fostering a healthier planet.

April 2022

In April 2022, Dreams Trust was officially registered at the Sub Registrar Office in Velliyanai under government registration number BK/IV/6/2022, marking a significant milestone in our journey. This registration solidifies our commitment to serving our people with integrity and transparency.

May 2022

In May 2022, Dreams Trust donated steel berow to the Government Primary School in South Mettupatti, enhancing their infrastructure and supporting educational endeavors. This contribution reflects our dedication to empowering local institutions and fostering community development.

April 2023

In 2023, Dreams Trust organized an E-Sevai camp, facilitating access to online public services for the peoples. This initiative aimed to empower individuals by providing convenient and efficient means to avail essential government services.

March 2024

Donated RO purifier to Thirumalainathanpatti Government Primary School, enhancing access to clean drinking water for students. Ensuring a healthier learning environment and promoting student well-being. Together, we're investing in a brighter future for the community.

Our Focus

  • Education
    At Dreams Trust, we believe in the power of education to transform lives. Through our various educational initiatives, we aim to provide access to quality education for all, regardless of their socio-economic background. From distributing school supplies to organizing educational programs and workshops, we are committed to empowering individuals through knowledge.
  • Plantation
    Environmental sustainability is at the heart of our mission at Dreams Trust. We recognize the importance of preserving our planet for future generations, which is why we actively participate in plantation drives and tree-planting campaigns. By promoting green initiatives and raising awareness about the importance of trees, we strive to create a greener and healthier environment for all.
  • Health
    Ensuring access to healthcare services is essential for the well-being of communities. At Dreams Trust, we prioritize health and wellness by organizing health camps, providing medical assistance to those in need, and promoting healthy living practices. addressing healthcare disparities and improving access to medical resources, we aim to contribute to the overall health and vitality of our society.
  • Social Work
    Social responsibility lies at the core of our values at Dreams Trust. We are dedicated to serving marginalized communities and addressing social issues through various outreach programs and initiatives. From providing humanitarian aid to supporting vulnerable populations, we strive to make a positive impact and foster a more inclusive society.
  • Women and Child development
    Empowering women and nurturing the growth and development of children are fundamental to our work at Dreams Trust. We are committed to promoting gender equality, advocating for women's rights, and providing resources and support for women and children in need. Through education, healthcare, and skill-building programs, we aim to create brighter futures for women and children, ensuring they have the opportunity to thrive and succeed.
